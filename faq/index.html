Technical Standards

Why do you mount hangers into the rafters?

+

Standard fascia-only mounting is prone to failure under ice loads. We use heavy-duty hidden hangers secured directly into the rafter tails using extended-grip stainless fasteners. This creates a structural bond that prevents gutters from pulling away during Pennsylvania winters.

What sealant do you use for mitered corners?

+

We avoid cheap hardware-store silicone. We use industrial-grade polyurethane sealant that retains high-tension elasticity through 200-degree temperature swings. This ensures your corners remain leak-free during extreme freeze-thaw cycles.

Do you handle underground drain lines?

+

Every professional cleaning includes high-pressure downspout verification. If we detect a blockage in your underground corrugated or PVC lines, we provide a technical report to help you protect your foundation from water intrusion.
