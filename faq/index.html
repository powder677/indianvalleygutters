Cleaning & Pricing

How much does gutter cleaning really cost? +

We believe in flat-rate transparency. For a standard 1-story home (Ranch/Bungalow), it is $99. For a standard 2-story home (Colonial), it is $149. This includes debris removal, downspout flushing, and a structural audit. Larger estates or homes with 3+ stories are quoted via satellite imagery.

What is included in the "Structural Audit"? +

While we clean, we check 12 structural points, including: loose spikes ("screw-creep"), leaking miter corners, detached downspout straps, and fascia rot. If we find an issue, we will note it on your invoice with a repair price‚Äîbut we never do work without your permission.

Do you clean the underground drains? +

We ensure water flows freely into the ground drain. However, if the underground pipe itself is crushed by roots or clogged deep underground, you will need a plumber with a camera snake. We stop at ground level.

Repair & Engineering

Why do you use Stainless Steel screws? +

Standard zinc or aluminum screws react with the acidity in oak leaves and rust within 3-5 years. We use 316-Marine Grade Stainless Steel. It costs us more, but it means your gutters will never fall off the house again due to rusted fasteners.

Can you fix a gutter that is pulling away from the house? +

Yes. This is called "Screw-Creep," caused by freeze-thaw cycles. We fix it by removing the old spikes and installing Hidden Hangers that are screwed directly into the roof rafters (not just the fascia board) for maximum hold.

Gutter Guards

Why don't you install plastic gutter guards? +

Plastic and vinyl warp in the PA summer heat (95¬∞F+). When they warp, they create gaps that let debris in but trap it so you can't clean it out. We only install rigid aluminum frames with stainless steel mesh.

Will your guards stop pine needles? +

Yes. We use a "Micro-Mesh" with a 0.027-inch aperture. This is tight enough to block Lansdale pine needles and roof grit, but open enough to let water flow through even during heavy thunderstorms.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Amex, Mastercard) via our secure digital invoice link. We also accept checks for commercial jobs.
