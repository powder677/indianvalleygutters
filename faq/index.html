Technical Standards

Why do you mount hangers into the rafters?

+

Fascia boards are decorative, not structural. In PA winters, the weight of ice can rip fascia right off the house. We use heavy-duty hidden hangers secured directly into the rafter tails using 316 stainless steel fasteners for a permanent structural bond.
