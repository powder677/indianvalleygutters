In the Indian Valley, a gutter system is only as durable as its mechanical connection to the home's skeleton.

The Fastener Protocol: 316-Grade Stainless

Standard aluminum or zinc-plated screws are the leading cause of system detachment in Pennsylvania. Due to thermal hydraulic expansion, water trapped behind a gutter freezes and expands by 9%, exerting massive outward force.

We solve this using extended-grip stainless fasteners. Unlike zinc, which oxidizes and causes "rust-jacking" (splitting the wood grain), 316-grade stainless is chemically inert. Our fasteners utilize a serrated thread pitch that creates a permanent mechanical lock, eliminating fastener creep.
