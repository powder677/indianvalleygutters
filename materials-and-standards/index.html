The Fastener Standard

In Montgomery County, bi-annual freeze-thaw cycles rip standard aluminum screws out of fascia boards. We refuse to use them.

Extended-Grip Stainless Steel

We utilize 316-grade stainless steel fasteners. These penetrate 1-inch deeper than industry standard, reaching the structural sub-fascia. The self-locking threads prevent the "screw-creep" caused by the weight of Pennsylvania ice dams.

Technical Warning: Standard aluminum screws work loose within 36 months. When water freezes in the fastener hole, it expands the wood grain. Only locking stainless threads maintain structural grip in PA winters.

Structural Rafter Mounting

Fascia boards are decorative, not structural. Mounting gutters only to the fascia is a guaranteed recipe for sagging and "water-trap" pockets.

Precision Hanger Placement

Our heavy-duty hidden hangers are driven through the fascia and directly into the rafter tails. This transfers the weight of water and snow directly to the home's framing.
