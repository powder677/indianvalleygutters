The Materials & Methods of Filtration

In the Pennsylvania climate, generic plastic screens fail within 24 months due to UV brittleness and expansion-contraction cycles. To achieve "maintenance-free" status in towns like Souderton or Harleysville, the hardware must meet specific metallurgical standards.

Micro-mesh Technology (The Atlas Standard)

We utilize 316 Stainless Steel micro-mesh. Unlike standard aluminum mesh, 316-grade is surgical steel that resists the corrosive tannins found in oak leaves. The 0.027-inch aperture is scientifically sized to allow surface tension to pull water into the trough while shedding even the smallest maple "helicopters."
