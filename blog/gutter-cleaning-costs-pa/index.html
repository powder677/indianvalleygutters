Why Traditional Estimating Fails

Most contractors in the Indian Valley area base prices on "linear feet." This is a flawed metric. In PA, the cost isn't in the length of the metal; it's in the technical difficulty of the debris load (oak tassels vs. pine needles) and the height safety requirements.

The Montgomery County Biomass Challenge

Whether you are in Doylestown or Souderton, our mature maple and oak canopies produce a dense organic sludge. We account for this biomass in our flat rate. We don't upcharge for "extra leaves" because we know exactly what to expect from a Pennsylvania roofline.
