Fine Debris & Silt

While guards stop large leaves, pine needles, pollen, and roof granules often sneak through the mesh. Over time, this creates a "sludge" layer inside the gutter that blocks the downspout exit.

The Angle Factor

In many local Souderton homes, guards are installed at too flat an angle. Instead of debris blowing off, it sits on top, gets wet, and creates a water-resistant mat.
