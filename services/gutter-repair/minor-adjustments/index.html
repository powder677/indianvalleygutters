The Failure Escalation Cycle

Ignoring a minor leak in a town like Souderton or Harleysville triggers a predictable and expensive failure sequence:

  1. Saturation: Persistent dripping saturates the fascia board grain beyond its drying capacity.
  2. Biological Growth: Damp wood creates an entry point for Gloeocapsa Magma and structural pests.
  3. Structural Rot: Fasteners lose their "bite" as wood fibers break down.
  4. System Failure: The gutter detaches under the static weight of a Pennsylvania winter ice load.
