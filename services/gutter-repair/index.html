Structural Restoration

We don't just patch leaks; we restore the structural integrity of your home's drainage using rafter-mount engineering.

The Technical Repair Standard

In Pennsylvania, the bi-annual freeze-thaw cycle is the primary cause of gutter failure. When water freezes in a clogged system, the expansion force pulls standard hardware out of the wood. We fix this by bypassing decorative fascia and driving extended-grip 316-grade stainless fasteners directly into the structural rafter tails using heavy-duty hidden hangers.
