The "Technical Clean" Protocol

1

Manual Biomass Extraction

We don't use leaf blowers that blast mud all over your siding. We hand-scoop the wet "sludge" created by decomposing Red Oak and Maple leaves.

2

Hydro-Static Downspout Flush

A clean gutter is useless if the downspout is clogged. We snake and flush every downspout to ensure water exits at least 4 feet from your foundation.

3

12-Point Structural Audit

We check for "screw-creep" (loose spikes), miter leaks, and pitch alignment. If we find a problem, we provide a quote—but we never upsell you on things you don't need.

Common Questions

Do I need to be home? ▼

No. As long as we have access to the backyard and water spigot, we can perform the work. We will email you a photo receipt upon completion.

How do I pay? ▼

We accept all major credit cards via our secure online portal. You will receive a digital invoice after the job is done.

Do you clean underground drains? ▼

We ensure water flows *into* the ground drain. If the underground pipe itself is crushed or clogged with roots, we can recommend a specialist, but we stop at the ground level.
