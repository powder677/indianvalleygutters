What is a Technical Audit?

Most companies "blow and go." They blow the leaves out and leave. We don't. Every Indian Valley Gutters service includes a Structural Audit. We physically check the 316 Stainless Fasteners and verify that your system is still anchored to the structural rafter tails, not just the decorative fascia.

In Pennsylvania, the freeze-thaw cycle creates screw-creep. If we catch it during a cleaning, it's a 5-minute fix. If you ignore it, it's a $2,000 gutter replacement. We prioritize your home's skeleton over a quick paycheck.
