Loose Gutters After Winter?
We Re-Secure Them.

Why it happens: In Montgomery County, water expands by 9% when it freezes. This force pushes standard gutter spikes out of the wood ("screw-creep").

Our Solution: We don't just hammer them back in. We remove the loose spikes and install 316-Stainless Steel Hidden Hangers screwed directly into the rafter tails. This ensures your gutters can handle the next winter.

The "Screw-Creep" Failure

If you can see the silver nail heads sticking out of your gutter, the structure is compromised. A 20-foot run of ice-filled gutter weighs ~250 lbs. Standard spikes cannot hold this lateral load.

  • ❌ Spikes pull out of rotting fascia
  • ❌ Water flows behind the gutter
  • ❌ Pitch is lost (standing water)

Common Questions

Can you fix the gutter without replacing it? ▼

Yes. As long as the aluminum trough isn't crushed or bent, we can "re-hang" it. We remove the old hardware and install new brackets that bite into fresh wood.

Do you use zinc or stainless screws? ▼

We exclusively use 316-Marine Grade Stainless Steel. Standard zinc screws rust within 2-3 years in PA weather. Ours are guaranteed for the life of the gutter.
