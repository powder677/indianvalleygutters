Frequently Asked Questions

Do gutter guards really work?

Yes! Atlas Gutter Guards use micro-mesh technology that has been proven to block 99% of debris while allowing maximum water flow. They're particularly effective against leaves, pine needles, and roof granules that commonly clog gutters in Pennsylvania.

Will I ever need to clean my gutters again?

Atlas Gutter Guards dramatically reduce maintenance needs. Most homeowners never need to clean their gutters again. In rare cases, surface debris may accumulate on top of the guards, but this simply brushes off naturally or can be blown off in minutes.

Can gutter guards handle heavy rain?

Absolutely. Atlas Guards are designed to handle even Pennsylvania's heaviest rainstorms. The micro-mesh design allows for superior water flow while keeping debris out, actually improving your gutter system's performance.

Will installation damage my roof or void my warranty?

No. Our professional installation method securely attaches guards to your gutters and fascia without penetrating your roof membrane or shingles. This protects your roof warranty while ensuring a secure, long-lasting installation.

How long do Atlas Gutter Guards last?

Atlas Guards are built to last 20-30+ years. The 316 stainless steel construction won't rust, corrode, or deteriorate, even in harsh weather conditions. Many homeowners consider them a permanent home improvement.

What if I need to clean my gutters in the future?

Atlas Guards can be temporarily removed if needed for any reason and then reinstalled. However, this is rarely necessary due to their superior debris-blocking performance.

Are gutter guards worth the investment?

Yes. Between eliminating recurring cleaning costs ($300/year), preventing water damage, increasing home value, and providing peace of mind, gutter guards typically pay for themselves within 3-5 years while lasting 20-30+ years.
