Telford FAQ

Do you clean high barn gutters? ▼

Yes. We have 40ft ladders and stabilizers specifically for high peaks on barns and farmhouses. We can also install commercial-sized downspouts for larger roof surface areas.

How do you handle the mud in the gutters? ▼

We manually scoop the heavy sludge first (so it doesn't clog your underground pipes), and then we flush the system with high-pressure water to clear the residue.
