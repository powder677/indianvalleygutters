Harleysville FAQ

Do you have insurance for 2-story homes? ▼

Yes. We carry full liability and workers' compensation insurance specifically rated for ladder work up to 3 stories. Don't risk a handyman on a 24-foot ladder.

Can you fix gutters on vinyl siding? ▼

Absolutely. Vinyl siding often hides rot. We use "stand-off" stabilizers to ensure our ladders never crush your siding while we reinforce the fascia behind it.
