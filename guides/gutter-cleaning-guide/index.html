1. Top 5 Signs Your System is Failing

If you see these signs, you aren't just looking at a clog—you are looking at active structural damage.

The "Waterfall" Effect

Water cascading over the front lip means the downspout elbow is 100% impacted with an organic plug.

Fastener Creep

Silver nail heads sticking out from the gutter? The weight of wet debris is pulling the spikes out of the fascia.

Biological Colonization

Saplings growing in the trough indicate a layer of decomposed soil sediment that adds extreme weight.

Siding Efflorescence

Chalky white stains on siding suggest water is leaking *behind* the gutter due to seal failure.
