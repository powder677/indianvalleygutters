Why Most Gutter Cleaning Companies Frustrate Homeowners

Living in Souderton, PA, I've discovered that most gutter cleaning companies operate with the same frustrating business model:

  • Estimate-based pricing that requires scheduling multiple visits
  • Surprise upcharges for "unexpected" debris or minor repairs
