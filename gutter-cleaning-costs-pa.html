Gutter Cleaning Costs in Bucks & Montgomery Counties (2025 Guide)

If you're a homeowner in Bucks or Montgomery County, you're probably wondering: "What are the average costs for professional gutter cleaning services near me?" With Pennsylvania's varied weather patterns, regular gutter maintenance isn't just recommended—it's essential.

The Bottom Line on Pricing

Professional gutter cleaning in this area ranges from $149-$199. A 1-story home is $149 and a 2-story home is $199. This price includes a full cleaning of all gutters and downspouts, debris removal, and an inspection of your system, with no hidden fees.

What Factors Affect Gutter Cleaning Costs?

While we use a simple flat-rate system, it's helpful to understand the factors that traditionally influence gutter cleaning prices in our area:

  • Home Size & Height: The number of stories is the biggest factor, determining the equipment and safety precautions needed.
  • Local Environmental Factors: Our area has many mature oak and maple trees, which create significant debris in the fall and spring.
  • Gutter Condition: The severity of clogs and the presence of gutter guards can impact the time needed for a thorough cleaning.
